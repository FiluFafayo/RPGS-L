HOW TO PLAY

GAMEPLAY BASICS

1. Roll dice (2d6) to move on the board
2. Use Action Points (AP) to modify your roll
3. Land on event tiles to trigger story events
4. Make choices that affect your journey
5. Complete quests and defeat bosses for Victory Points
6. Reach an ending tile to complete your adventure

CHARACTERS

Choose from 4 races (Human, Elf, Dwarf, Halfling) and 4 classes (Warrior, Rogue, Mage, Cleric). Each combination gives unique stats and abilities.

THE BOARD

The board has 120 tiles across 5 regions: Forest, Mountain, City, Dungeon, and Wasteland. Each region has unique events and challenges.

EVENTS & CHOICES

When you land on an event tile, you'll face a situation with multiple choices. Some require skill checks (roll d20 + stat vs DC).

WINNING

Gain Victory Points (VP) through quests, defeating enemies, and making good choices. Reach an ending tile with the most VP to win!
